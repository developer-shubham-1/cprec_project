About Us

Center for Policy Research and Consultancy (CPReC) is a research institute registered in Nepal that works in the socio-economic development of Nepal by providing policy-related consultancy services and launching advocacy for policy change.

CPReC has established working relationships with:

  • Government of Nepal (Federal, Provincial, and Local levels)
  • International organizations & donors
  • National-level organizations & networks
  • Community-based NGOs across Nepal

We specialize in impact evaluation, data collection, and stakeholder empowerment through a wide range of research and development projects.
